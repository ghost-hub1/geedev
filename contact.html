Common Questions

Frequently asked.

How much does a website cost?+
It depends on what you need. A simple landing page starts around $200-$300. A full multi-page business website runs $400-$600. More complex projects like e-commerce or dashboards are quoted individually. I'll always give you a clear price before any work begins.
How long does it take?+
Most websites take 5-10 days depending on complexity. Landing pages can be done in 3 days. I'll show you a draft halfway through so you can give feedback before the final version. Rush delivery is available for an extra fee.
Will my website work on mobile?+
Every single site I build is mobile responsive from the start. It will look and work perfectly on phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops. I test on multiple screen sizes before delivering.
Do you do website redesigns?+
Absolutely. If your current site is outdated, slow, or just doesn't look good anymore — I can modernize it. Send me the URL and I'll give you an honest assessment plus a quote. Check out my before/after showcase to see examples.
What do you need from me to start?+
Your logo (if you have one), photos, the text content for your pages, and any websites you like the look of for reference. Don't worry if you don't have everything — I can work with bullet points and help you put the content together.
How does payment work?+
Simple and safe — 50% upfront to start, 50% when you're happy with the final result. I accept PayPal, Wise, and Payoneer. On Fiverr and Upwork, payment is handled securely through the platform.
