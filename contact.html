Common Questions

Frequently asked.

How much does a website cost?+
A landing page starts around $200–$300. A full multi-page business website runs $400–$600. More complex projects like e-commerce stores or dashboards are quoted individually. I always give you a clear price before any work begins — no surprises.
How long does it take?+
Most websites take 5–10 days depending on complexity. Landing pages can be done in 3 days. I show you a draft halfway through so you can give feedback before the final version. Rush delivery is available for an extra fee.
Will my website work on mobile?+
Every single site I build is mobile responsive from day one. It will look and work perfectly on phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops. I test across multiple screen sizes and browsers before delivering.
How does payment work?+
Simple and safe — 50% upfront to start, 50% when you're happy with the final result. I accept PayPal, Wise, and Payoneer. On Fiverr, payment is handled securely through the platform.
Do you do website redesigns?+
Absolutely. If your current site is outdated, slow, or just doesn't represent your business well anymore — I can modernize it completely. Send me the URL and I'll give you an honest assessment plus a quote.
What do you need from me to start?+
Your logo (if you have one), photos you want on the site, the text content for each page, and any websites you like the look of for reference. Don't have everything ready? No problem — I can work with bullet points and help you put the content together.
