I'm a full-stack web developer with a deep focus on creating digital products that don't just look good — they perform. Every website, every app, every widget I build is designed to solve a real problem for a real business.

My background spans frontend and backend development, with a particular edge in security-conscious coding. I don't use templates or page builders. Every line of code is written by hand, optimized for speed, and built to last.

I believe your website should be your hardest-working employee — bringing in customers 24/7, building trust before you ever pick up the phone.

When I'm not building websites, I'm creating custom iOS widgets that push what's possible on iPhone home screens — live data, premium design, automated workflows. It's the intersection of utility and beauty that drives me.

I work remotely with clients across the US, UK, Canada, and Australia. Whether you're a restaurant owner in Houston or a startup founder in London — if you need something built right, let's talk.
